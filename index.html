One thing that bandlab and my guitar amp have are presets meant to emulate specific eras, artists, or moods, which inspired the approach I took for this meditation.

I started out by identifying the moods I wanted to create: Happy, Sad, and Evil. For Happy, I went for an upbeat, bouncy feel by using chorus freeverb, and pitch shift and adding faster playback speed to enhance the energy. For Sad, I slowed the track down and used a combination of filter, reverb, and delay to create a more mellow tone. For Evil (my favorite of the three), I wanted something chaotic and a bit unsettling, so I incorporated bitcrusher, distortion, and tremolo while slightly reducing the playback speed to 0.9. Overall, I really enjoyed playing around with the effects of this meditation and felt like I learned a lot about how I'd like to use effects for my own musical projects going forward.
